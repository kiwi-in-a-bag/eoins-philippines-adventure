Mar 31

San Manuel Rice Farm

📍 Tarlac / Pangasinan area

We stayed right on a working farm. It was amazing to see how much work goes into growing the rice we eat at home!

💡 Fun Fact for the Class:

Most rice in the Philippines is still planted by hand! Farmers stand in water to push the seedlings into the mud.

Apr 8

Talisay & Taal Volcano

📍 Club Balai Isabel

The resort was right on the water. We could see the Taal Volcano, which is a volcano inside a lake!

💡 Fun Fact for the Class:

Taal is one of the smallest active volcanoes in the world, and it has its own crater lake inside it!

Current Stop

Daet, Bicol

📍 Camarines Norte

We are here now! The waves are big because Daet is famous for surfing. We are also trying very spicy food.

💡 Fun Fact for the Class:

Bicol is famous for "Bicol Express," a dish made with lots of chili and coconut milk. It's named after a train!
